Our Company

Professional logistics solutions built on experience and innovation

OMNI is a modern city logistics company, originating from the 3PL services sector. We were created to cover the critical last-mile segment, offering the upgraded solutions that today's market demands.

With a steadfast commitment to proper, professional, and legally compliant delivery, OMNI continuously invests in human resources, expertise, and equipment, strengthening the sales and customer service of our partners.

The Company

OMNI is a 3PL spin-off company, leveraging deep industry experience and offering comprehensive city logistics services. We serve market demands with high-quality services, full compliance with new legislative frameworks, and absolute professionalism at every stage of delivery.

Who is OMNI

OMNI was founded on decades of experience in the logistics sector. We have specialized personnel with experience from multinational company projects and a fleet of high-specification vehicles.

We are committed to sustainability, investing in new trucks as well as fully electric vehicles up to 3.5 tons, reducing our carbon footprint and complying with new European directives for urban distribution.

Operating from a local Hub, we cover X-docking project needs and provide the necessary immediacy in connecting the Thriasio Plain with the urban fabric.

The Hub can function as an Urban Consolidation Center (UCC), in accordance with European practices and modern city logistics standards.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

📦 What areas do you deliver to?

We deliver across all of Greece including islands, with same-day service in Athens metro area. We also offer international shipping to 20+ European countries and worldwide through our partner network.

💰 How is shipping cost calculated?

Shipping costs depend on weight, dimensions, distance, and service type. Use our Quote Calculator above for instant estimates, or contact us for exact pricing. We offer volume discounts for regular customers.

🕐 What are your delivery times?

Same-day delivery in Athens, 1-2 days nationwide, 3-7 days for international shipments. Express and time-definite options available for urgent deliveries.

🛡️ Is my shipment insured?

Yes! All shipments include €100 basic insurance at no extra cost. Additional insurance up to €50,000. See our Insurance Information section for details.

📋 What items cannot be shipped?

We cannot transport hazardous materials (except with special permits), illegal items, perishables without proper packaging, or items exceeding our vehicle capacity. Contact us if you're unsure about your shipment.

🚚 Do you offer recurring delivery services?

Absolutely! We provide scheduled delivery services for businesses with regular shipping needs. Enjoy volume discounts, dedicated account management, and flexible scheduling. Contact us to set up a corporate account.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 21, 2025

1. Information We Collect

OMNI Logistics collects information necessary to provide our transportation and logistics services, including contact details, shipment information, and billing data. We use this information solely for service delivery and customer support.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to process shipments, communicate delivery updates, provide customer support, and improve our services. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as required for service delivery.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All sensitive information is encrypted and stored securely in compliance with Greek and European data protection regulations (GDPR).

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at ik@omnids.gr to exercise these rights.

Terms of Service

Last updated: November 21, 2025

1. Service Agreement

By using OMNI Logistics services, you agree to these terms. We provide transportation and logistics services as described in our service offerings and quotes.

2. Customer Responsibilities

Customers must provide accurate shipment information, proper packaging, and ensure all items comply with transportation regulations. Prohibited items will not be transported.

3. Payment Terms

Payment is due as specified in the service agreement. Late payments may result in service suspension and additional charges.

4. Limitation of Liability

OMNI Logistics liability is limited to the declared value of the shipment as specified in our insurance policy. See our Insurance Information for details.

Shipping Policy

Last updated: November 21, 2025

1. Delivery Times

Standard delivery times vary by service type and destination. Same-day delivery available in Athens metro area. Express nationwide delivery 1-2 business days. International shipping 3-7 business days depending on destination.

2. Tracking

All shipments include real-time GPS tracking. You will receive tracking information via email and SMS. Track your shipment 24/7 through our website or mobile app.

3. Packaging Requirements

Items must be properly packaged to withstand normal transportation conditions. Fragile items require special packaging. We offer packaging services for an additional fee.

4. Refused or Undeliverable Shipments

If a shipment cannot be delivered, we will contact you to arrange redelivery or return. Additional charges may apply for storage and redelivery attempts.

Insurance Information

Last updated: November 21, 2025

1. Standard Coverage

All shipments include basic insurance coverage up to €100 per shipment at no additional cost. This covers loss or damage during normal transportation.

2. Additional Insurance

Higher value shipments can be insured for their full declared value. Premium insurance rates are 2% of declared value. Maximum coverage available: €50,000 per shipment.

3. Claims Process

To file a claim, contact us within 7 days of delivery with photos and documentation. Claims are typically processed within 14 business days. Approved claims are paid within 30 days.

4. Exclusions

Insurance does not cover improper packaging, prohibited items, acts of nature, or items not declared on the shipping manifest. See full terms for complete exclusion list.

Questions about insurance? Contact our team at ik@omnids.gr or call +30 693 707 7778 for personalized assistance.
